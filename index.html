My College Journey

College has been a mix of challenges and growth. I've learned to balance academics, work, and personal life while exploring new interests. One thing that stands out is how visual learning has helped me improve my communication skills. Working in groups has shown me what a real project could look like. It’s also cool to see everyone else have similar struggles and goals.

After graduating, I want to pursue a career in Interior design & Real Estate. I enjoy creating visually compelling compositions and bringing ideas to life through digital art and layout design.
